Our Mission

Constantly Connecting is a movement to inspire people to build meaningful connections with others, no matter their anxieties or circumstances. By encouraging daily acts of kindness, curiosity, and engagement, we aim to make the world a more connected and compassionate place.

The Story Behind Constantly Connecting

Founded by Colby Martel, a creator with a passion for human connection, this project is a reflection of his personal journey overcoming social anxiety to become a beacon of inspiration for others. Our Goals

- Inspire 1 million acts of kindness worldwide through daily challenges.
- Build a supportive global community that celebrates connection.
- Demonstrate that meaningful conversations can start with a simple question.
- Highlight the stories of individuals who are breaking down social barriers.
